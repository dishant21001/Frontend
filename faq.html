Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your weather forecasts?

Our weather forecasts are highly accurate, leveraging advanced meteorological data and state-of-the-art forecasting models. We continuously update our forecasts to reflect the latest atmospheric conditions, aiming for the highest precision possible. However, like all weather predictions, our forecasts are based on probabilities and can be subject to changes due to the unpredictable nature of weather patterns.

Do you provide hourly forecasts?

Yes, Weather Brainiac offers detailed hourly forecasts. These provide temperature, precipitation, wind speed, and humidity predictions for the next 48 hours, allowing you to plan your day with greater precision. Our hourly forecasts are updated regularly to ensure the latest and most accurate information

How can I get weather alerts?

You can sign up for weather alerts through our website. Once subscribed, we'll send you real-time alerts via email or SMS for severe weather events like storms, high winds, or extreme temperatures in your selected areas. This service is designed to keep you informed and prepared for any significant weather changes.
